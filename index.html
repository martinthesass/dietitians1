Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost?
A one off consultation is €80. I also offer a 10 week block. This is €500 which is more suited to people with longer term goals. More information HERE
Can I claim money back from my private health insurance?
This will depend on your insurance company as all companies will differ. Please check with your insurance company first. Many insurance companies do allow you to claim back a certain percentage of fees paid. All paid consultations will be issued with a receipt you can present to your insurance company if you need.
I don't have private medical insurance can I still claim money back?
Yes. Regardless of whether you have private insurance or not you should be entitled to claim 20% of any fees paid. This can be applied through revenue and claimed at the end of the year.
I am not sure if I am ready to work with a Dietitian - What does the Free call involve?
The free call is exactly that - a free call to discuss your goals or ask any questions to see if a Dietitian would be a right fit for you. It is really just an informal chat with no pressure to commit to anything. Here is how it works: Book your free consultation HERE.
Why should I trust a Dietitian? How do I know your'e not some snake oil merchant just telling me to eat avacados and lettuce all day?
Good question! All Dietitians in Ireland need to be registered with the governing body CORU for their profession. Think of it as quality assurance to protect the public from harm. All Dietitians are on the CORU register and you can check the register HERE. Just pop in my CORU ID DI054186 or search my name "Martin Cosgrove".
