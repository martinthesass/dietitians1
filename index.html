Ask a Question

Can I afford to work with a Dietitian?
Everyones budget is different but you might consider the cost in the long term if you don't work with a Dietitian! Think about how many times you may have wasted money on supplements, gym memberships you did not use or the latest fad diet. Now think how good it would be to get expert advice and reach your weight loss goals in the quickest time possible. That is certainly worth considering as a good investment.
I don't have the time to diet and exercise for weight loss
Most clients that work with us commit to doing 3 gym sessions per week for 45 mins to 1 hour per session. They commit to increasing their step count gradually throughout the process and gradually changing their diets and lose weight week on week. Some people do more and some less but you certainly don't need to be in the gym 7 days a week! Small changes done right make a huge difference and with less effort than you would expect.
I have tried diets in the past and never had any long term success - why will this be any different?
Unfortunately diets only work when your dieting and most people can only "diet" for short periods of time. To make a lasting change it has to be easy enough that you can continue for long periods of time with scheduled breaks. We pride ourselves on designing nutritional plans that are sustainable and fit with the clients lifestyle. We teach you how to eat for weight loss and not an overly restrictive diet that is only sustainable in the short term. Can you eat whatever you want and as much as you want and still lose weight? No - but you can certainly have some of your cake and eat it too! Why not book your free consultation and we can explain our process.
I am not sure if I am ready to commit - what does the free consultation involve?
The free consultation is exactly that - a free consultation with absolutely no pressure to commit to anything. It is really just a chat to discuss goals and see if a Dietitian would be a right fit for you. Here is how it works: Book your free consultation HERE. The Dietitian will then send you a questionnaire to understand more about what they can help with. The Dietitian will discuss this questionnaire with you in a video call or over the phone whatever you prefer. If you decide we are the right for you then you can proceed to sign up for a programme. If not that is absolutely fine. We never pressure anyone to enlist on any of our programmes - we are just here to help.
What if I am not happy with the service after signing up?
We think you will be very happy with the service from your initial consultation until you get your desired results but if at any time you are not happy for any reason we offer a full refund on your investment no questions asked! Just email us and tell us you are not happy and how we can improve and we will offer 100% refund - no problama amigo!
